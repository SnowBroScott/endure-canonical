## Purpose of This Page

This page defines hybrid identity, emergence, and drift in Endure.

Classes & Identity (v2) defines the foundational layer.
Hybrids operate as a **progression layer** — the shape sustained behavioral reality produces over time.

This separation is intentional and preserved.

---

## Foundational Relationship

Classes answer:

> **How do you endure?**

Hybrids answer:

> **What have you become because of how you endured?**

Hybrids are not alternatives to classes.
They are **consequences of sustained behavioral reality across two paths**.

---

## Core Hybrid Principles

### 1. Hybrids Emerge. They Are Not Achieved.

Hybrids are not unlocked.
They are not selected.
They are not rewards waiting at the end of a long enough streak.

They emerge when the world recognizes a behavioral truth that has already been accumulating.

The player does not choose a hybrid.
The system does not award one.

A hybrid is a **recognition** — the world naming what the player has already become.

---

### 2. Hybrids Represent Synthesis, Not Combination

Hybrids are **not**:

- Stat blends
- Dual classes
- Loadouts
- Talent forks
- Upgrades

They are **identity shifts** — a new shape produced by the sustained overlap of two distinct paths.

A hybrid does not say:

> "I do two things."

It says:

> "I am something that could only come from both of these."

---

### 3. Foundational Class Identity Is Never Replaced

Original class identity remains legible.
Origin path continues to matter.
Hybrid identity carries the traces of both paths that produced it.

Hybrids carry a stable name and a primary and secondary path.
Visual expression shifts dynamically within the blend — reflecting which path is currently dominant.

Hybrids deepen identity. They do not erase it.

---

## The Weighted Scale (Player-Facing)

Hybrid emergence is governed by a cumulative weighted scale that tracks behavioral reality across all four paths.

The player never sees this scale directly.
The player sees what it produces.

**How the scale behaves:**

- It accumulates over time and is never reset
- Inactivity does not move it; absence does not erase it
- Early behavior carries less weight than sustained long-term patterns — the scale is skeptical of early signal and trusts what endures
- A primary path confirms when behavioral weight is decisive and sustained
- Hybrid emergence begins when two paths are genuinely and consistently present — not as brief experimentation, but as lived behavioral reality
- Visual expression within the hybrid reflects current weight distribution between the two paths

The scale is not visible to the player as a number, percentage, or bar.

The player experiences the scale as **emergence** — the world reflecting what their effort has already made true.

### The Scale's Numerical Architecture Is Internal

The 99-point rolling window is an internal implementation mechanism, logged in the Session 5 decision record.

It is not player-facing and must never appear in player-facing language.

Players experience identity as emergence and drift — the behavioral pattern the scale is tracking, not the tracking itself. The numerical architecture exists to make the system honest and consistent. Its job is to be invisible.

---

## The Traveler Phase and Scale Accumulation

The weighted scale begins accumulating during the Traveler phase.

Early entries count. They move the scale. But the trust accumulation model means early behavior carries less cumulative weight than sustained long-term patterns — the scale is skeptical early, trusting over time.

Hybrid emergence cannot occur during the Traveler phase because hybrid status is defined relative to a confirmed primary path. No primary path exists during the Traveler state, so no hybrid threshold can be established. The Traveler phase is not a prerequisite delay — it is a genuine boundary condition. Hybrid emergence requires something to synthesize against.

But those early entries remain in the database permanently and age through the rolling window normally. Early effort is not discarded when a primary path confirms. It is incorporated into the scale's full history.

The Traveler phase is not outside the scale.
It is the scale's earliest chapter.

## The Six Hybrid Identities

Six hybrid identities exist.
Each is the confluence of two base class paths, sustained until the world recognizes the synthesis.

All six are peers.
None is rarer, higher, or more difficult to reach than a base class.
The path to any of them is simply honest effort across two ways of enduring.

---

### **Björn — Strider + Forgeborn**

Movement carrying mass and inevitability.

The Björn does not move quickly or powerfully alone.
They move in a way that contains both — grounded momentum, distance measured in deliberate weight.
Flow does not soften the force. Force does not stop the flow.

Effort is felt as something that cannot be turned aside.

---

### **Vargr — Strider + Unbroken**

Relentless motion under pressure.

The Vargr does not pace themselves against resistance.
They meet it and keep moving — not through it, but with it and past it.
Endurance and intensity fold into each other until stillness becomes impossible.

Effort is felt as something that does not know when to stop.

---

### **Haukr — Strider + Mystic**

Rhythm elevated into discipline.

The Haukr does not move for distance or control alone.
They move as practice — breath and cadence refined until motion becomes ritual.
Precision and flow arrive at the same place from different directions.

Effort is felt as something that has been made sacred through repetition.

---

### **Naut — Forgeborn + Unbroken**

Pressure meeting chaos.

The Naut does not channel force carefully or release it recklessly.
Both are present simultaneously — density and volatility, heat barely contained.
The effort does not resolve. It continues, barely held together.

Effort is felt as something that has been forged out of refusal.

---

### **Hjörtr — Forgeborn + Mystic**

Weight made intentional.

The Hjörtr does not exert force without awareness or control without substance.
Presence and precision arrive in the same movement — deliberate, unhurried, irreversible.
The load is held with full understanding of what it costs.

Effort is felt as something that has been chosen completely.

---

### **Naðr — Unbroken + Mystic**

Intensity without loss of self.

The Naðr does not burn wildly or move cautiously.
Chaos and discipline coexist — the storm understood and directed rather than suppressed.
Endurance becomes stillness at speed.

Effort is felt as something that has learned to contain itself.

## Ten Peers

Endure has ten identity paths.
Four base classes. Six hybrids.
All ten are peers.

This is not aspiration. It is architecture.

No identity is higher. No path leads to a better destination.
Hybrid identity confers no progression advantage.
Hybrid status does not gate access to harder content.
A hybrid and a single-path player doing equivalent real-world effort share equal access to what that effort unlocks.

Hybridization is identity reflection, not capability threshold.

The distinction matters:

- A player who reaches Naut is not more advanced than a Forgeborn
- A player who remains Strider is not less developed than a Vargr
- Every one of the ten paths is a complete way of enduring

If any language implies hierarchy between them, it is wrong.

---

## Blueprint Pools: Ten Distinct, None Additive

Each of the ten identities has its own distinct blueprint pool.

Hybrid pools are not additive. Björn does not inherit Strider pool content and Forgeborn pool content. Björn has its own pool — its own construction content, its own visual identity, its own thematic character — specific to that hybrid and no other.

Access to any pool requires current behavioral alignment with that identity. The mechanism is identical across all ten. No hierarchy. No inheritance. No coverage advantage.

A hybrid player has access to **different** blueprints, not **more** blueprints.

Equal breadth. Distinct content.

This is why "hybrid identity confers no progression advantage" holds cleanly under this architecture. The blueprint pool reflects who the player currently is — not how far they have traveled or how many paths they have touched. The Hold accumulates the full journey in its structures, but access at any moment is a reflection of current identity, not a reward for identity history.

## Signals Before Confirmation

Hybrid emergence is preceded by **signals**, not announcements.

The player should suspect transformation before the world confirms it.

Possible signal types (directional, not prescriptive):

- Recurrent visual motifs during effort that do not belong to a single path
- Descriptive language beginning to drift between two registers
- Momentary manifestations during peak exertion carrying the character of both paths
- Environmental or clan responses that seem to acknowledge something the world has not yet named

The system recognizes the pattern before it names it.
The player recognizes it before the system confirms it.
Confirmation arrives when both are already true.

---

## Drift: The Other Ceremony

Hybrid status is not permanent.

Sustained neglect of one path within a blend causes drift — the scale reflecting a behavioral reality that has changed.

Drift is not failure. It is honesty.

**How drift feels:**

- Signals appear before drift is confirmed — the same way signals appear before emergence
- Familiar hybrid visual expressions begin to simplify or shift toward one path
- The world does not accuse; it reflects

When drift completes, the player returns to the identity their sustained behavior actually describes.

**Earning and drift carry equal ceremonial weight.**

The emergence sequence and the drift sequence are the same kind of moment — the world recognizing what is true. One is not triumphant and the other punishing. Both are honest. Both are complete.

Returning to a pure path is not a step backward.
It is a return to a different shape of endurance — no less valid, no less mythic.

### Drift and the Weighted Scale

Drift is not a separate mechanism.

It is the weighted scale doing exactly what it always does.

The 99-point rolling window reflects current behavioral reality. Old entries age out as new ones come in. A player who earned hybrid status through sustained behavioral overlap across two paths — then shifts sustained effort back toward a single path — will see those secondary path points gradually age out of the window. When the secondary path falls below the hybrid emergence threshold, hybrid status resolves.

The scale did not reset. Nothing was taken. Behavioral reality shifted, and the scale reflected it.

Drift is emergence running honestly in reverse. The mechanism is identical.

The scale never resets. Absence does not move it. Only sustained behavior does — in either direction.

## Aspect & Transformation Direction

Hybrid expression may involve **temporary aspect states** during peak exertion.

Guiding constraints:

- Mostly humanoid forms
- Symbolic exaggeration — presence, posture, mass, intensity — rather than literal transformation
- Manifested during peak effort or mastery states; never cosmetic
- Always tied to exertion

Visual expression within a hybrid reflects current weight distribution between the two paths.
As behavioral balance shifts, visual expression shifts with it.

Transformation is not decoration.
It is **effort made visible** — the accumulated weight of a behavioral truth expressed in form.

---

## Design Constraints

- Hybrids must never feel like achievements, unlocks, or rewards
- Hybrid emergence must feel inevitable in retrospect — not climactic in the moment
- No hybrid identity should overshadow any base class in mythic weight, visual impact, or perceived desirability
- The drift sequence must be presented with the same ceremonial care as the emergence sequence
- Player-facing language must never reference scale thresholds, percentages, or progress bars
- Absence and inactivity must never trigger or accelerate drift

If a hybrid feels like a prize, it is wrong.
If it feels like a recognition, it is right.

If drift feels like punishment, it is wrong.
If it feels like honesty, it is right.
